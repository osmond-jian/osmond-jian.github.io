My Services

I am dedicated to making sure that my clients recieve completed projects that exceeds their expectations. This is how I work towards that goal.

Consultation

Everything begins with a conversation to better understand the spirit of the project. This includes things such as: the purpose of the website, features that the website should have, colour schemes, etc.

Proposal & Creation

I will draft a detailed proposal based on a budget that will fully disclose and outline all the major steps involved in creating the website. During the creation process, I regularly update my clients on the progress of their project.

Launch & Support

After launch, I offer training to allow clients to easily make content changes. I also provide ongoing support after project launch in case anything goes wrong afterwards, so my clients can have peace of mind.
